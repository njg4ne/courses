Nicholas Gardella

Nicholas is a PhD Student in the Department of Systems & Information Engineering at University of Virginia. He works under Dr. Sara Riggs in the Riggs Lab within the Human Systems Engineering Research Laboratory at UVA. He is also a member of the Link Lab. To see personal projects and features on GitHub, please visit https://njg4ne.github.io/

Nicholas earned his B.S. in Computer Science from Virginia Tech in 2021 and his M.Eng. in Systems Engineering from UVA in 2023. To browse his undergraduate and graduate course grades, please navigate to Education.

Nicholas is an NSF Graduate Research Fellow, and NSF National Research Trainee in Cyber-Physical Systems. His research area is Human-AI interaction in computer programming.
